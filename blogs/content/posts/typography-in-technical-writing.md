## Reading as an Interface

For a technical blog, the text is the UI. If the typography is poor, the content suffers—no matter how brilliant the insights are.

### The SF Pro Stack

Apple's **SF Pro** is a masterpiece of modern typography. It's designed for legibility across all sizes and weights, making it perfect for technical documentation.

| Property | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Line Height | 1.5 - 1.6 |
| Content Width | 680px - 720px |
| Primary Font | SF Pro Text |

### Hierarchy and Rhythm

Establish a clear path for the eye. H1 should be bold and commanding, while body text should have enough "breath" (whitespace) to prevent cognitive fatigue.

> "Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a durable visual form." — Robert Bringhurst

## Final Thoughts

Invest in your type. It's the most direct way to elevate your brand and ensure your message is actually read.
